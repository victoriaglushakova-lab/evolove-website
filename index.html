Our story

Beauty as a
practice

Evolove was born at an intersection I've lived in for years — between the psychology of inner transformation and a deep love of beauty, form and meaning.

After eight years in fashion at Calvin Klein, and years of work as a life coach and NLP practitioner, I found myself drawn to creating something that held both worlds at once. Not jewellery that merely decorates, but pieces that carry genuine intention — anchors for the states we choose to live from.

Every piece is made by hand, in Amsterdam. Each one begins not with a design, but with a feeling.

— Victoria
